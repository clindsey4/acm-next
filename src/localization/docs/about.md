## The ACM
The ACM, or Association for Computing Machinery, brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. 

We are the Murray State University chapter of the ACM, hosting various events and activities related to computer science and more on campus.

## Rewards Program
Every semester, our ACM chapter runs an awards program to win an Amazon gift card.

Whenever attending a meeting, participants will receive a set number of points, adding to their accumulated total over the course of the semester. The person with the most points will receive the gift card at the end of the semester.

If you are a chapter member of the ACM, you will receive extra points per meeting towards your total.

To view your current attendance points total, visit the [account page](../account).
